# Revvy Learn — Marketing Strategy

## Part 1: Google Ads Campaign (£20/month Budget)

### Budget Reality

£20/month (~£0.66/day) is an extremely tight Google Ads budget. Average CPCs in education range £0.50–£3.00, meaning you'll get roughly **7–40 clicks per month**. The strategy below maximises every penny.

### Recommended Campaign Setup

**Campaign Type:** Search (NOT Performance Max — too little data at this budget)
**Bidding Strategy:** Maximize Clicks (not conversion-based — need 30-50 conversions/month for smart bidding)
**Geographic Target:** United Kingdom only
**Language:** English
**Ad Schedule:** 4pm–10pm weekdays + weekends (peak student study hours)
**Device:** All devices (students are heavily mobile)

### Keyword Strategy (Exact Match Only)

Focus on 5–8 hyper-specific long-tail keywords with high intent and low competition:

**Primary Keywords (exact match):**
- [edexcel a level economics revision]
- [edexcel a level business revision]
- [edexcel economics revision app]
- [a level economics revision tool]
- [edexcel international a level revision]

**Secondary Keywords (exact match):**
- [a level economics revision website]
- [edexcel business studies revision app]
- [free a level economics revision]

### Negative Keywords (Add Immediately)

These prevent wasted spend on irrelevant clicks:
- GCSE, BTEC, IB, AQA, OCR, Cambridge
- teacher, teaching, jobs, salary
- textbook, book, PDF, download
- university, degree, masters
- free (if you want paying users only — keep if free trial is your funnel)

### Ad Copy

**Ad 1 — Economics Focus:**
- Headline 1: Edexcel A-Level Economics Revision
- Headline 2: Interactive Quizzes & Past Papers
- Headline 3: Free Trial — Start Revising Today
- Description 1: Master Edexcel Economics with our smart revision app. Glossary, command words, past papers & blackjack-style quiz games.
- Description 2: Built for International A-Level students. Track progress and master every topic. Try free now.
- Display URL: revvylearn.com/economics

**Ad 2 — Business Focus:**
- Headline 1: Edexcel A-Level Business Revision
- Headline 2: Smart Quizzes & Exam Prep Tools
- Headline 3: Start Your Free Trial Today
- Description 1: Revise Edexcel Business with interactive tools. Glossary, command words & gamified learning to boost your grades.
- Description 2: Everything you need for exam success. Past papers, key terms & progress tracking. Join free.
- Display URL: revvylearn.com/business

### Landing Page

Direct ads to the **homepage** (https://revvylearn.com) or create a dedicated landing page with:
- Clear value proposition above the fold
- "Start Free Trial" CTA button
- Social proof (if available — student testimonials)
- Subject overview showing what's covered

### Monthly Optimisation Routine

| Week | Action |
|------|--------|
| Week 1 | Review search terms report, add negative keywords |
| Week 2 | Check CTR — pause ads below 2% CTR |
| Week 3 | Review which keywords got clicks, pause non-performers |
| Week 4 | Adjust bids, test new ad copy variation |

### Budget Allocation

- **Month 1:** £20 on economics keywords only (test one subject)
- **Month 2:** If economics works, split £10 economics / £10 business
- **Month 3+:** Double down on whichever subject converts better

### Scaling Advice

At £20/month you're essentially gathering data. Once you see which keywords convert:
- Increase to £50–100/month to get meaningful traffic
- Consider Google Ads Grants for education (up to $10,000/month free for eligible nonprofits)
- Explore YouTube ads (cheaper CPV, great for student audience)

## Part 2: Organic Growth Strategy

### 1. Content Marketing (Highest Impact — Free)

**A. Blog / Resource Pages**
Create SEO-optimised content targeting what students actually search for:

| Content Type | Example Topics | Search Intent |
|-------------|---------------|---------------|
| Revision guides | "Edexcel Economics Theme 1 Summary" | Informational |
| Exam tips | "How to answer 25-mark economics questions" | Informational |
| Command words | "Evaluate vs Analyse A-Level Economics" | Informational |
| Past paper walkthroughs | "Edexcel Economics 2024 Paper 1 Answers" | Informational |
| Subject comparisons | "Economics vs Business A-Level — which is easier?" | Navigational |

**B. Topic Cluster Strategy**
Build pillar pages for each subject with supporting content:

```
PILLAR: Edexcel A-Level Economics Complete Guide
  ├── Theme 1: Introduction to Markets and Market Failure
  │   ├── Supply and Demand Explained
  │   ├── Market Failure Types & Examples
  │   └── Government Intervention Methods
  ├── Theme 2: The UK Economy
  │   ├── GDP, Inflation & Unemployment
  │   ├── Fiscal & Monetary Policy
  │   └── International Trade & Globalisation
  ├── Theme 3: Business Behaviour
  └── Theme 4: A Global Perspective
```

**C. Content Calendar**
- Publish 1–2 blog posts per week
- Target long-tail keywords with low competition
- Update content before exam season (April–June)

### 2. Technical SEO (Already Started)

**Completed:**
- ✅ Sitemap.xml created and submitted
- ✅ robots.txt configured
- ✅ Google Search Console verified
- ✅ Meta tags in place

**Still To Do:**
- [ ] Add structured data (Schema.org) — `EducationalOrganization`, `Course`, `FAQPage`
- [ ] Ensure all pages have unique meta descriptions
- [ ] Add Open Graph tags for social sharing
- [ ] Optimize images with alt text
- [ ] Ensure Core Web Vitals pass (check in Search Console after data collects)
- [ ] Add internal linking between related pages

### 3. Social Media (Free — High Effort, High Reward)

**TikTok & Instagram Reels (Priority #1 for students)**
- Post 3–5 short revision tip videos per week
- Format: "Did you know?" economics facts, 60-second topic summaries
- Use trending sounds + education hashtags
- Link to RevvyLearn in bio

**YouTube (Priority #2 — Long-term SEO value)**
- Create topic walkthrough videos (5–15 min)
- Past paper walkthroughs get massive search volume before exams
- Videos rank in Google Search AND YouTube Search

**Reddit (Priority #3 — Community)**
- Engage genuinely in r/6thForm, r/ALevel, r/Edexcel
- Answer questions, share free resources (don't spam links)
- Build reputation then mention RevvyLearn naturally

### 4. Community & Partnership Building

**A. Student Forums & Communities**
- The Student Room (TSR) — UK's largest student forum
- Reddit A-Level communities
- Discord study servers

**B. Teacher & School Outreach**
- Contact A-Level economics/business teachers directly
- Offer free classroom accounts
- Teachers recommending your tool = organic word-of-mouth

**C. Educational Partnerships**
- Reach out to Edexcel/Pearson community resources
- Guest post on education blogs
- Collaborate with A-Level YouTube creators

### 5. Email Marketing (Free with tools like Mailchimp)

- Collect emails via free trial signup
- Send weekly revision tips during term time
- Increase frequency during exam season
- Share new features and content updates

### 6. Referral Programme

- "Invite a friend, both get 1 week free premium"
- Students naturally study in groups — leverage this
- Add a share button within the app
